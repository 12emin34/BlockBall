Change the skin of the ball

After this setup your ball will have got a different appearance.

  • 1. Open your arena menu
  • 2. Enter the name of the skinowner or enter a custom skin url /Settings/Ball design/appearance/Set Ball skin

    Samples: 'Shynixn' or 'http://textures.minecraft.net/texture/edf84715a64dc45586f7a6079f8e49a9477c0fe96589b4cfd71cba32254ac8' If the url is too long for the chat, simply enter it into the arena_x.yml file.

  • 3. Save your arena /Save arena
  • 4. Join a game and check if the skin of the ball has changed
