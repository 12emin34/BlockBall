Join via signs [Hubgame]

After this setup players will be able to join games by just running into the forcefield. Teams get selected automatically. Please check if you have already set an alias for the arena, otherwise the id will be shown on the sign. Click here if you want a name instead of an id.

  • 1. Place 2 signs anywhere on your server. One for the redteam, one for the blueteam.
  • 2. Open your arena menu
  • 3. Use /Settings/Lobbygamemode settings/Set redteam sign
  • 4. Now rightclick on your already placed sign for the redteam
  • 5. Use /Settings/Lobbygamemode settings/Set blueteam sign
  • 6. Now rightclick on your already placed sign for the blueteam
  • 7. Save your arena /Save arena
  • 8. Join the game by clicking the redteam-sign or the blueteam-sign
