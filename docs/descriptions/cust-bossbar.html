Bossbar

After this setup your players will obtain a bossbar during a game where they can find the time left and amount of goals each team has scored.

BossBar for Minecraft-Version 1.8.x

BossBar for Minecraft-Version 1.9.x and above
  • 1. Open your arena menu
  • 2. Move to /Settings/Annoucements/Set bossbar/[Requires +1.9.x] Bossbar
  • 3. Use Toggle bossbar to enable the bossbar for your game
  • 4. [Optional] Change the bossbar design by using the provided options
  • 5. [Optional] Use Set message and enter your wanted text.The following placeholders are available:
    • :countdown - Time left
    • :red - Name of teamred
    • :blue - Name of teamblue
    • :redcolor - Color of teamred
    • :bluecolor - Color of teamblue
    • :redscore - Score of teamred
    • :bluescore - Score of teamblue
  • 6. Save your arena /Save arena
  • 7. Join the game and check if the bossbar pops up when the ball spawns
BossBar for Minecraft-Version 1.8.x
  • 1. Make sure you installed the plugin BossBarAPI
  • 2. Open your arena menu
  • 3. Move to /Settings/Annoucements/Set bossbar/[Requires BossBarPlugin] Bossbar
  • 4. Use Toggle bossbar to enable the bossbar for your game
  • 5. [Optional] Use Set message and enter your wanted text.The following placeholders are available:
    • :countdown - Time left
    • :red - Name of teamred
    • :blue - Name of teamblue
    • :redcolor - Color of teamred
    • :bluecolor - Color of teamblue
    • :redscore - Score of teamred
    • :bluescore - Score of teamblue
  • 6. Save your arena /Save arena
  • 7. Join the game and check if the bossbar pops up when the ball spawns
