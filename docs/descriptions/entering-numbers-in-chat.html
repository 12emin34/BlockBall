Entering numbers in chat does not work?

It is often the case that some Chat-Plugins especially Bungeecord Chat-Plugins do not handle chat messages correctly to be used with BlockBall. There are 2 options to solve this issue:

Navigate via commands

This is the recommend option because you just have to change something in the BlockBall config.yml and does not interfere with other plugins.

  • 1. Open the config.yml of BlockBall and scroll down to navigate-chatmenu
  • 2. Set enabled to true . Click here to find out more about dynamic commands
  • 3. Reload or restart your server. This setting cannot be applied via the blockballreload command
  • 4. Open the chat menu via /blockball and start navigating by entering /bbc [number]
Uninstall the Chat-Plugin

This might only be a good option if you really do not need the Chat-Plugin at all.

  • 1. Uninstall your Chat-Plugin
  • 2. Reload or restart your server
